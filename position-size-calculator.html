Crypto Position Size Calculator

Decide how much to risk on each trade and let the math tell you how big your position should be – based on your stop loss and account size.

Open the position size calculator

Why position sizing matters

  • Protects your account from large drawdowns.
  • Keeps your risk per trade consistent (for example 1–2%).
  • Helps you survive long losing streaks.
  • Works for spot, futures and other leveraged products.

How to use this position size calculator

  1. Enter your total account size in USD or your local currency.
  2. Choose how much you want to risk on this trade (e.g. 1%).
  3. Set your entry price and stop-loss price.
  4. On the main page calculator you’ll see the correct position size.

Over time, consistent position sizing can be more important than finding the “perfect” entry, especially in volatile crypto markets.
