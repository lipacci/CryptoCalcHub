What is DCA?

Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) means investing a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, instead of trying to time the perfect entry.

This strategy can help smooth out volatility and reduce the emotional pressure of buying dips and tops.

How to use this DCA calculator

  1. Type an optional asset name (for example BTC or ETH).
  2. Enter how much you invest each period (week, month, etc.).
  3. Set the number of periods and your average buy price.
  4. Click Calculate DCA result on the main page.

The tool will estimate your total invested amount, total coins accumulated and the current value based on the live or manual price.
