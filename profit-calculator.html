How to use this profit calculator

  1. Choose a coin or token (e.g. BTC, ETH, SOL – optional field).
  2. Enter how much you plan to invest in USD or your local currency.
  3. Type your entry price and the expected exit price.
  4. Click Calculate profit to see your result in money and percentage.

You can also use it as a “what if” tool: try past prices or future targets and see how much you would have made if you bought earlier, or what a future move could be worth.

Why this tool is useful

  • Makes risk/reward visible before you enter a trade.
  • Helps you avoid chasing pumps without knowing the downside.
  • Works with any crypto or token – just type the prices.
  • Completely free, no login, no tracking.
