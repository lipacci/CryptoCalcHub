Quick examples – How it works

  1. Profit Calculator – You buy BTC at 30,000 with an investment of 1,000. Later the price is 35,000. The calculator shows your quantity of BTC, final value and profit (around +16.6% in this example).
  2. DCA Calculator – You invest 100 every month for 12 months, average buy price 25,000 and current price 30,000. The tool shows total invested, accumulated quantity and your current profit on the whole plan.
  3. Position Size Calculator – Account size 1,000, risk per trade 1%, entry price 30,000, stop-loss 29,000. The calculator tells you how many units you can buy so that a stop-loss hit costs only 10 (1% of your account), plus the notional value of that position.
